About

Here at UVA, we are driven, ambitious, and bold.

The UVA Sustainability Commission [https://sustainability.virginia.edu/about-us/plans-progress] aims to make UVA carbon neutral by 2030 and fossil fuel free by 2050. Meeting these sustainability goals to address the environmental impact of our campus requires us to be united and motivated to alter the way we pursue our education here so that it reflects our commitment to the planet.

Image Credit: https://sustainability.virginia.edu/

The challenges of climate change require us to build a community of changemakers willing to demonstrate their commitment to the fight with action. These changemakers will lead the transition of making our lifestyles sustainable. Elevating our community will require a coordinated effort that is only possible when users like you are willingly to put in that work.
